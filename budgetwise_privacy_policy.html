Introduction

BudgetWise AI ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile app and related services.

Information We Collect

How We Use Your Information

  • Generate AI-powered financial insights.

Data Sharing

Your information is never sold. Limited sharing occurs only with:

  • Stripe: For secure subscription billing.
  • HubSpot: For transactional and onboarding emails.
  • Cloudflare Workers: For secure backend processing.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption, salted password hashing, HTTPS-only communication, and secure token-based authentication.
